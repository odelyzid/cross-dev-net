68k / Amiga 500, 2000, and A1200-class TCP client
---------------------------------------------

Primary implementation (same protocol as POSIX) lives under:

  ../amiga/mixnet.c
  ../amiga/README.md

This folder keeps short cross-helpers and keyword notes for 68000/AmiTCP. For
line framing shared with 8-bit serial bridges, also see:

  ../genesis/mixnet_line.c

The generic portable client remains:

  ../posix/mixnet.c   (use on Linux, macOS, WSL, or Amiga+ixemul)
